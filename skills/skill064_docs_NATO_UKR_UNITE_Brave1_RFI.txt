## [3] OPERATIONAL WORKFLOW (THE 8-STAGE PROCESS)

The system shall track progress through the following mandatory stages:
- **Stage 0**: creating database of companies
- **Stage 1**: initial compliance of companies
- **Stage 2**: matchmaking of verified companies from UA and NATO Allies
- **Stage 3**: Joint proposal from UA and NATO Allied companies submitted via Brave1 web portal application page
- **Stage 4**: NATO-Ukraine evaluation process, including pitches on joint project proposals
- **Stage 5**: issuing contracts
- **Stage 6**: implementation of contracts
- **Stage 7**: Opportunities for technology validation in NATO and testing via TEST in UKRAINE programme
- **Stage 8**: final report & audit
