# CONTEXT: Visual Identity & Design System Governance | Status: DRAFT 2026-03-29

## [0] METADATA (ADR)
- Problem: Alignment of digital interfaces with NATO’s 2025 visual identity and military standards.
- Decision: Adopt the "NATO Blue" palette and bilingual (NATO-OTAN) layout requirements with the NATO 2026 visual identity (Blue/White/Black)
- Mechanism: Mapping of the "NATO Star" (Compass Rose), specific color palette and official reporting styles.
- Sample branding from NATO The Secretary General’s Annual Report 2025, located at https://www.nato.int/content/dam/nato/webready/documents/publications-and-reports/annual-reports/sgar25-en.pdf
- Consequence: High-fidelity brand representation for Allied platforms. Sovereign-grade UI consistency for Allied communication platforms.

---

## [1] BRAND IDENTITY (NATO)
- **Primary Logo**: https://www.confianza23.es/assets/images/logotipo.png
- **Secondary Logo**: The NATO compass rose (star) divided into four quadrants with white structural lines.
- **Bilingual Mandate**: All primary branding must display "NATO" and "OTAN" with equal visual weight.
- **Core Slogan**: "Protecting Allied territory and populations".
- **Visual Tone**: Professional, authoritative, and focused on collective defense.
- **Placement**: Header (Top-Left) with proportional scaling.
- **Visual Assets Directory**: `assets/images/` 

---

## [2] DESIGN SYSTEM (NATO)
- **Framework**: "NATO Blue" from "One NATO" Branding, as https://www.nato.int/en/about-us/official-texts-and-resources/use-of-nato-content-and-brand
- **Core Palette (Official 2025 Colors)**:
  - **NATO Midnight Blue (Primary)**: Deep blue used for headers and branding blocks.
  - **NATO Tactical Blue (Accent)**: Medium-light blue used within the compass rose quadrants.
  - **Sovereign White**: Pure white for the compass star and high-contrast typography.
  - **Command Black**: Solid black backgrounds for bilingual text blocks in high-visibility logos.
- **Others, from Mistica**:
  - **Mistica Blue (Primary)**: #001935 (Headers, Surfaces, Filled Icons).
  - **Mistica Accent (Action)**: #00a9e0 (Regular Icons, Links, CTA).
  - **Background (Surface)**: #f0f4f8 (Light Blue-Grey).
- **Typography**:
  - **Headlines**: Modern, bold Sans-Serif (uppercase) for sections like "FOREWORD" and "ANNEXES".
  - **Body Text**: High-legibility Serif fonts for long-form reporting and official documentation.

## [4] IMPLEMENTATION CONSTRAINTS
- **Icon Styling**:
  - `Regular`: Use `stroke: #00a9e0` (stroke-width: 1.5).
  - `Filled`: Use `fill: #001935`.
- **Transitions**: Smooth (cubic-bezier) 0.3s.
- **Interactions**: On-Hover `translateY(-5px)`
- **Security**: Mandatory sanitization of HTML if using `unsafe_allow_html=True` for SVGs
